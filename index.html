About Me

Hello! I'm Kimani Anthony, a passionate designer, photographer, first aider/trainer, and web developer with over 5 years of experience. My journey began with a simple love for capturing the beauty of everyday moments, and it has since evolved into a full-fledged career. I specialize in multiple disciplines and I'm dedicated to creating visuals that tell a story, saving lives through first aid, and building engaging web experiences.

I've had the privilege of working with numerous clients, from individuals to large organizations, helping them bring their visions to life through creative design, photography, first aid training, and web development. Let's work together to create something amazing!
